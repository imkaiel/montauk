Project Montauk


Introduction

Montauk is a front-end framework in development. It aims to provide a reliable and consistent design that efficently scales on all devices. More importantly, it emphasizes a 'humanist' visual language reflected it's timeless and user-friendly design.

Purpose

  • Synthesize principles of human design on our endless endevor to evolve public perception of the contemporary history.

  • To create great experiences that shape the way people move through the world.

  • Develop a single underlying system that allows for a fundamentally unified experience.
